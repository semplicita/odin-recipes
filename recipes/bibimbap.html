Bibimbap

Description

Bibimbap is a Korean rice dish. The term "bibim" means "mixing" and "bap" refers to cooked rice.

Ingredients

  • white rice
  • sautéed vegetables
  • sliced meat
  • a fried egg sunny side up
  • sweet-spicy-savoury Bibimbap sauce

Steps

  1. Cook the rice
  2. Throw all the other ingredients in
  3. Bon appétit
